Bir-Billing: Cleanup 254

Little did I know, a seed of thought of doing a clean-up in Bir planted by @thereisnoearthb would materialize into something so meaningful.

Really appreciate all you beautiful people for turning up and coming together at such a short notice. Taking abundance of hope & learning from here :)
